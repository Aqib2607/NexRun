# 1. Brand Identity

## Brand Positioning

NexRun represents:

* Performance
* Motion
* Precision
* Confidence
* Modern athletic culture

The visual experience should feel similar in quality to:

* Nike
* Adidas
* Gymshark
* Lululemon

but must maintain its own visual identity.
